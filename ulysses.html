Ulysses

Link Github

Ulysses is a padrino built site to display interviews with tech entrepreneurs geared towards Italian youth. The video is at the forefront of the website, acting as the center of visual interest and a preview of the interview.

The content of the site is controlled through an admin dashboard. The admin can edit the videos, upload the background pictures to S3, and moderate the comments.
